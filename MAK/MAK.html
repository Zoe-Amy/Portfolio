From Sound to Form
Movement becomes line, melody becomes colour, harmony becomes form. Through the layering of shapes, words emerge whose colours carry emotions and work together harmoniously. The poster translates what one hears and feels into a visual language. In the animation, the idea is expanded: letters build up, chapters close, a graphic conclusion inspired by the end of studies and the beginning of a new chapter.
