Kinetic Typography
Can type be more than just a carrier of meaning? In this project, text becomes a living organism that embodies its own message. Letters disintegrate, dance, stumble, they become what they express. FEHLER breaks apart, loses its order and becomes the error itself. NERVEN KOSTÜM VERLEIH vibrates in nervous repetition; the letters become jumbled like thoughts before a major performance. The animation is not decoration but interpretation. Every movement and transformation reinforces the statement of the words and makes their meaning visibly experiential.
