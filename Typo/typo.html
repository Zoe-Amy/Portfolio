Staged Typography
This project focused on developing three-dimensional letter sculptures using cardboard and wire. The physical construction demanded a deep understanding of materiality to stabilise the fragmented, geometric forms. The subsequent photography became a discipline of lighting technique and perspective control. Through shadows and deliberate light accents, the volume and texture of the pale forms were emphasised. The series explores how the chosen perspective dynamises the perception of static objects within space.
Project
